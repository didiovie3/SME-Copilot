# Design System: Uruvia Mobile Dashboard
**Project ID:** 10329087592518136477

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The Uruvia Mobile Dashboard is designed with a **"High-Trust Business Command"** vibe. It is clean, functional, and organized into clear, actionable cards. The aesthetic is modern and vibrant, utilizing high-contrast typography and a punchy primary blue to instill a sense of precision and professionalism. It prioritizes data clarity while maintaining a premium mobile-first experience.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
* **Signal Blue (#135BEC):** The primary brand color. Used for primary call-to-actions, category icons, and highlighting the focus area of the dashboard.
* **Pure Surface White (#FFFFFF):** The background color for all high-level cards to ensure maximum readability and separation from the page background.
* **Canvas Gray (#F8FAFC):** The subtle background of the application that creates "air" between the card components.
* **Prosperity Green (#10B981):** Highlights revenue, net income, and positive growth trends.
* **Expense Rose (#EF4444):** Used for highlighting expenses and urgent alerts that require immediate attention.
* **Obsidian Black (#0F172A):** Used for main headers and critical KPI values to ensure a bold, authoritative hierarchy.
* **Slate Muted (#64748B):** Used for secondary labels, "Copilot Insights," and less critical metadata.

## 3. Typography Rules
* **Font Family:** **Inter** (Global sans-serif).
* **Headings:** Bold to Black (700-900 weight) with tight tracking to create a strong, "command" feel.
* **KPI Values:** Large, bold font sizes (2XL+) used for currency values like ₦156,650,000.
* **Body:** Clean, legible weight (400-500) for insights and labels.

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Cards/Containers:** **ROUND_EIGHT** (8px) corner roundness. Subtle, whisper-soft diffused shadows to create depth without visual clutter.
* **KPI Pulse Cards:** Full-width width with a light background wash (5% opacity of the brand color) when used for summary stats.
* **Strategic Goals Section:** Uses progress bars with rounded caps and high-contrast labels.
* **Operational Alerts:** Uses icon-led rows with bold headers and muted descriptions.
* **Bottom Navigation:** Clean, icon-led navigation bar with a solid backdrop and active states highlighted in Signal Blue.

## 5. Layout Principles
* **Vertical Flow:** Mobile-first layout with clearly defined card boundaries.
* **Whitespace Strategy:** Generous padding within cards (approx. 16-24px) and clear gutters between sections (approx. 24px) to prevent cognitive overload.
* **Hierarchy:** "Performance Pulse" (KPIs) always sits at the top, followed by "Strategic Goals" (Progress), then "Operational Alerts" (Action items).
